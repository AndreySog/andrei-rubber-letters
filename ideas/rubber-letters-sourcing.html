RFQ текст поставщику

Hello. We need custom illuminated letters made from white translucent silicone/rubber, not acrylic. Requirements: - 3D letter body, white when off, evenly glowing when LED is on. - RGB or single-color LED options. - Outdoor version: waterproof, UV-resistant, cold/heat resistant. - Anti-vandal concept: body should be soft/elastic or impact-resistant, not brittle. - Please confirm available Shore A hardness options: very soft / medium / hard. - Can you make fire-retardant silicone/rubber? - Can you provide impact test video: hammer hit, drop test, bending/stretching test? - MOQ for sample and small batch? - Price for sample letter 300 mm high and full word/logo 1 meter wide? - Production time and shipping options to Russia? Please send photos/videos of similar silicone/rubber illuminated signs and material certificates if available.

Перед оплатой требовать

  • Trade Assurance order only.
  • Видео фабрики/образца, не только рендер.
  • Материал: silicone/rubber type, Shore A hardness, UV resistance, fire-retardant option.
  • Outdoor rating: target IP65/IP67.
  • LED voltage, driver, warranty, repairability.
  • Heat test: LED внутри резины может перегреваться при плохой конструкции.
  • Impact/bending demo video на реальном материале.
  • Shipping quote to Russia and HS code.

Важно: обычные acrylic channel letters не являются продуктом Андрея. Дифференциатор — белый полупрозрачный rubber/silicone body + LED inside + impact‑resistant/flexible options.
